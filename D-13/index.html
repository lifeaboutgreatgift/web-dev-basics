Overflow Examples

overflow: hidden

This text is very long and will be cut off when it goes outside the box boundary. You cannot scroll to see it!

overflow: scroll

This text is very long and will be scrollable when it goes outside the box boundary. You can scroll to see all the content inside this box!

overflow: auto

This text is very long and scrollbar only appears when needed. This is the most commonly used overflow value in real projects!
